Cyclophosphamide Response Analyser using miRNA Expression

(CyRA-miR)

Using EasyEnsembleClassifier (EEC) model, this CyRAMIR tool utilizes expression profiles of microRNAs (miRNAs) from the Cyclophosphamide treated breast cancer patients samples to accurately stratify patients into two categories: responder and non-responder and predict their probability in terms of confidence score. This tool will aid in chemotherapy drug response prediction and help optimising the selection of appropriate therapeutic regimen for patients with breast cancer, thereby implementing patient-specific precision medicine.

# miRNA Expression

Your CSV should have 4 data rows (one per miRNA) with at least two columns: the miRNA name (as mentioned below) and its expression value.

miRNA,Expression
hsa-miR-136-3p,4.25
hsa-miR-21-5p,18.45
hsa-miR-1266-5p,3.88
hsa-miR-193a-3p,4.12

The first column is the miRNA name and the second column is the numeric expression level e.g., log2 (CPM+1).
